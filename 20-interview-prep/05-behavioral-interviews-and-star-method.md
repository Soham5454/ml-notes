## The STAR method — genuinely the standard, essential structure

```
Situation: genuinely brief context -- what was the real scenario?
Task: what was YOUR specific responsibility/goal in that situation?
Action: what did YOU SPECIFICALLY do? (Genuinely the most important
  part -- specific, concrete actions, not vague generalities)
Result: what GENUINELY happened? (Recap file 01's quantified-impact
  discussion directly -- specific, honest outcomes matter here too)
```

Genuinely worth understanding WHY this structure works, recap DSA file 19's problem-solving framework and ML System Design file 12's tradeoff-communication structure directly — the SAME underlying discipline running throughout this entire roadmap: STRUCTURED, SPECIFIC communication is consistently more convincing than vague, unstructured answers, whether describing a training-loop debugging session or a system design tradeoff or, now, a behavioral story.
