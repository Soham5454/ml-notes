## What an ATS actually is, and why it genuinely matters

```
ATS (Applicant Tracking System): software MOST companies use to
automatically SCAN and FILTER resumes before a human ever sees them --
genuinely important, real practical fact: a resume that looks great to
a HUMAN but confuses an ATS may NEVER reach a human reviewer at all
```

Genuinely worth understanding this changes some real, practical formatting decisions — ATS software genuinely parses TEXT, and can struggle with complex visual layouts (multi-column designs, tables, graphics, text boxes) — a genuinely important, real tradeoff between visual polish and machine-readability.
